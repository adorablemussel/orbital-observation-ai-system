DATASET DIFFICULTY ANALYSIS
===========================

1. Did pixel accuracy decrease?
Yes. More noise makes it harder for the model to see shapes.

2. Which class became harder?
The urban class. Small gray blocks look like random background noise.

3. Did small structures become more difficult?
Yes. Very small objects completely disappear.

4. What does this suggest about real Earth Observation data?
Real satellite images have clouds and sensor noise. Simple models will fail to find small buildings in real life.
