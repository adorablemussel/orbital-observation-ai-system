CLASS-WISE ACCURACY ANALYSIS
============================

1. Which class achieved the best accuracy?
Background and water. They are very large and simple.

2. Which class achieved the worst accuracy?
Urban areas. They are small and have sharp edges.

3. Is global pixel accuracy misleading?
Yes. The global accuracy is 99.45%[cite: 1454]. But this is mostly because the background is huge and easy to predict.

4. Why are small classes often harder to evaluate?
If a small object is shifted by just one pixel, its individual accuracy drops a lot. A one-pixel error on a big lake does not change much.
