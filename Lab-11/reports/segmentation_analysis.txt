SEGMENTATION ANALYSIS
=======================

Pixel accuracy:
0.9945 (99.45%)[cite: 1454].

Visual observations:
The model works very well. It found all objects correctly.

Most accurate class:
Water (blue) and background (dark gray).

Most difficult class:
Urban areas (light gray blocks). 

Boundary errors:
The predicted urban blocks have rounded corners. The ground truth has sharp, straight corners.

Small object errors:
Small urban blocks merge together into one big shape.

Engineering conclusions:
The Small U-Net learned to find objects very well and achieved high accuracy[cite: 1454]. However, pooling layers in the model lose sharp details of small structures.
